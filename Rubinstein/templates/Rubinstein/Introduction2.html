  • In each of the first five rounds, you will be asked, independently and simultaneously to request money amount between $5 and $50 in multiples of $5.

{$5, $10, $15,..., $50}

 

  • Your earnings consist of two parts.

- You can receive the money amount you request (the first part).

- You can receive extra money amount depending on your position (the second part).

1. If your position is A, there is no extra money you could receive.

2. If your position is not A :

a. You receive extra $25 if you request the same money amount as your opponent.

b. You receive extra $50 if you request the money amount that is exactly $5 less than the money amount that your opponent requested.
