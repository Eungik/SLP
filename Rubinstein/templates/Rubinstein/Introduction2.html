In each of the first five rounds, you are asked to pick a (integer) number between 11 and 20.

Your earnings consist of two parts.

You receive the number you choose (the first part).

You can receive extra earnings depending on your position (the second part).

1. If your position is A, there is no extra token you could receive.

2. If your position is not A :

a. You receive extra 10 tokens if you pick the same number as your opponent.

b. You receive extra 20 tokens if you pick a number that is exactly one less than your opponent.
