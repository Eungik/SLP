In this task, you will participate in 10 rounds of decision-making.

At the beginning of each round of this task, you will be randomly matched with four other individuals.

First Five Rounds

There are five network positions labeled A, B, C, D, and E, which is shown in the figure below. In each round, you are randomly assigned to one of the five positions. You will be assigned to each position once and only once in the first five rounds.

 

 

Your position determines who your opponent is.

If your position is A, your opponent is B.

if your position is B, your opponent is C.

if your position is C, your opponent is D.

if your position is D, your opponent is E.

if your position is E, your opponent is A.
