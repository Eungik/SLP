Task 2

The game varies depending on two variables.

1. The first type of variation introduced is whether a player in his turn can add 1 or 2 (K=2) or add 1, 2, or 3 (K=3).

2. The second type of variation introduced is whether the winning number (M) is 4, 6, 8, 10, 11, or 15.

 

You will have in total six variations of the game as follows:

(M, K) = (4,2), (6,3), (8,2), (11,3), (10,2), (15,3).
