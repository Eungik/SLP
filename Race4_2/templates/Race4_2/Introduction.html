Task 2

In this task, you will participate in 6 rounds of decision-making.

In each round, you are asked to play one of "six variations"of the following game against a computer player who is programmed to make every choice to maximize the chance of winning.

 

 

The two players, you and the computer, alternately choose how many "STEPS" to take.

You are the first mover, and in each turn one is allowed to take up K steps.

The player who gets to a "TARGET NUMBER" first wins the game.

If you win a game, you receive 5 SGD. Otherwise, you receive 0 tokens.
