Task 2 Round 1 (4,2) game

The two players, you and the computer, alternate: you move first, the computer moves next, you move again, and so on.

You always move first from 0, and in each turn one can add an integer number between 1 and 2.

The player who gets to 4 first wins the game

If you win a game, you receive 5 tokens. Otherwise, you receive 0 tokens.
