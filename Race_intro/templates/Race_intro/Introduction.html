You will participate in 6 rounds of interactive decision-making.

In each round, you are asked to play one of "six variations"of the following decision-making against a computer player who is programmed to make every choice to maximize the chance of each winning against you.

 

 

The two players, you and the computer, alternately take turns to choose how many "STEPS" to take.

You are the first mover, and in each turn one is allowed to take up to K steps (1,2,...K).

The player who gets to a "TARGET NUMBER" first wins the game.

If you win a game, you receive $5. Otherwise, you receive $0.

One of six rounds will be randomly selected for your earnings in Task II at the end of this module.
